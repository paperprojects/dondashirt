The Shirt

Inspired by Kanye 'Donda Listening Party' on 8/5/2021 in Atlanta's Mercedes Benz Stadium, we custom designed the 'Donda Shirt'. The 'DOND' is designed in 'THE LIFE OF PABLO' font and the 'A' is designed to represent Atlanta as it is the symbol of the 2021 World Champions: The Atlanta Braves.

The design for the back of the shirt came from the Atlanta Falcons tweet in which they wrote about Kanye wearing a custom Falcons jersey.
